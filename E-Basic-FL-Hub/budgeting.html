What is Budgeting?

Budgeting is the process of planning how to spend your money. It helps you prioritize expenses, save for future goals, and stay in control of your financial life.

Why Budgeting Matters

  • Helps track income and expenses
  • Prevents overspending and debt accumulation
  • Encourages saving and financial independence
  • Promotes long-term financial stability

How to Create a Budget

  1. Calculate your total monthly income
  2. List all your expenses
  3. Classify expenses as fixed, variable, or discretionary
  4. Set spending limits for each category
  5. Track progress and adjust as needed
