What is Credit?

Credit is the ability to borrow money or access goods or services with the understanding that you’ll pay later. It plays a crucial role in personal and business finance.

Why Credit Matters

  • Qualify for loans and credit cards
  • Get lower interest rates
  • Improve chances for rental approvals
  • Access better financial opportunities

How to Improve Your Credit

  1. Pay bills on time
  2. Keep credit card balances low
  3. Don’t open too many new accounts at once
  4. Check your credit report regularly
  5. Dispute any errors

Advantages of Credit

  1. Use for emergencies
  2. Buy now, pay later
  3. Purchase protection
  4. Helps establish good credit if used wisely

Disadvantages of Credit

  1. Overuse
  2. High interest/annual fees
  3. Increase your debt
  4. Establish poor credit if not used wisely

Tips to Stay in Control of Your Credit Card

  1. Use only one credit card
  2. Shop for the best credit card
  3. Consider a secured card – a credit card that requires a cash collateral deposit
  4. Don’t charge anything you can’t pay for
  5. Pay your monthly bill on time and in full
