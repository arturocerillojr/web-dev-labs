Why Financial Literacy Matters

Financial literacy empowers you to manage debt, build savings, understand credit, and plan for long-term goals like retirement or home ownership.

Key Financial Literacy Topics

  • Budgeting: Creating a plan for your income and expenses.
  • Saving: Building an emergency fund and short/long-term savings.
  • Credit: Understanding your credit score and how to improve it.
  • Investing: Growing your money through stocks, bonds, or mutual funds.
  • Debt Management: How to manage and reduce debt effectively.
