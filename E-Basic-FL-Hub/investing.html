Why Should You Invest?

  • Grow your wealth over time
  • Beat inflation and preserve purchasing power
  • Achieve long-term financial goals (retirement, education, etc.)
  • Generate passive income

Common Types of Investments

  • Stocks: Ownership in companies, with potential for high returns
  • Bonds: Loans to governments or corporations with fixed returns
  • Real Estate: Property investment for rental income or resale
  • Mutual Funds: Pooled investment managed by professionals
  • ETFs: Exchange-Traded Funds that track indices or sectors
