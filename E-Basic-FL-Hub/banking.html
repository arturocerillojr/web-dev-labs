What is Banking?

Banking refers to managing money and providing services like savings, loans, and digital access to funds. Banks help people securely save, invest, and borrow money for personal and business needs.

Common Banking Services

  • Savings Accounts: Store money safely and earn interest.
  • Checking Accounts: Daily use accounts with ATM/debit access.
  • Loans & Mortgages: Funds for cars, homes, or education.
  • Credit Cards: Borrow short-term and build credit.
  • Mobile Banking: Manage accounts from your phone or computer.

How to Choose the Right Bank

Consider location, fees, interest rates, mobile features, and customer service. Compare online banks with local branches for better financial convenience.
